Aircraft Stress Analysis

We undertake projects that require Structural Justification reports for Major and Minor Modifications and Repairs of Aircraft Primary and Secondary Structures as well as Aircraft Interiors (CS/FAR 23,25,27 and 29).

We have the expertise and resources to provide

Finite Element Analysis (for Internal and Interface Loads derivation as well as detail stressing)

Static analysis to design the modifications and repairs and to comply with the required regulations

Fatigue and Damage Tolerance Analysis to design the modifications and repairs and to comply with the required regulations

Preparation of structural substantiation reports and compliance demonstration reports
